How to Play Expandle

Expandle is a word-guessing game where words get longer as you progress. Challenge yourself to guess words of increasing length, starting from 3 letters and expanding up to 8 letters!

Game Rules

  • Start with 3-letter words and progress up to 8-letter words
  • You have 8 attempts to guess each word
  • All words are common English words
  • Each guess must be a valid word of the correct length

Color Feedback

Purple: Letter is correct and in the right position

Red: Letter is in the word but in the wrong position

Gray: Letter is not in the word

Scoring System

Attempt Points
First attempt10 points
Second attempt8 points
Third attempt6 points
Fourth attempt5 points
Fifth attempt4 points
Sixth attempt3 points
Seventh attempt2 points
Eighth attempt1 point

Special Features

  • Use the hint button to reveal one correct letter (costs 1 point)
  • Track your progress with the staircase display of completed words
  • Challenge yourself to reach the highest level and score

Tips

  • Start with common letters like vowels (A, E, I, O, U)
  • Look for patterns in word families
  • Use your previous guesses to inform your next attempt
  • Save hints for longer words when you really need them
  • Try to maintain a high score by guessing words in fewer attempts
